About Me

I will be enrolling in the Robotics Department at the University of Michigan (UMich) in the fall of 2024 as a Master's student. I am particularly interested in Agile Legged Locomotion, Reinforcement Learning, and Robot Design.

Before that, I graduated from Texas A&M University with a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering. Working at the Unmanned System Lab, lead by professor Srikanth Saripalli, I gained valuable research experience with an emphasis on hands-on real-world experiments with autonomous vehicles.

During my time at A&M, I developed a mini (1 ft) bipedal robot inspired by the Agility Cassie.
